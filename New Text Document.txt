<h3>Procedure</h3>
    
<ul>
    <li>Add oil to a heavy bottom pan, fry onions till golden brown. Set these aside.</li><li> Fry capsicums till they turn slightly soft.</li>
<li>Marinate paneer with the marination ingredients and set aside.</li>
<li>Add 1 tsp oil to water and bring it to a boil, add the rice and cook till al dente. Drain off the rice and set aside.</li>
